# DAY 3 

1. To filter in SQL, the 'WHERE' clause is used. It is usually used with COMPARISON OPERATORS.
2. Comparison Operators are used with the WHERE clause to help filter data. Examples of Comparison Operators used with the 'where' clause include 
- Greater than >
- Less than <
- Not equal to <>
3. Multiple Criteria Filter includes using the following terms with the 'WHERE' clause and they include; 'OR', 'AND' and 'BETWEEN'.
